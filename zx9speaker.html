Upgrade your sound system with all new ZX9 active speaker. It's a bookshelf speakers system that offers truly wireless connectivity - creating new posibilities for more pleasing and practical audio setups.

Connect via Bluetooth nearly any wired source. This speaker features optical, digital coaxial, USB-Type-B, stereo RCA, and stereo XLR inputs, allowing you to have up to five wired source device connected for easy switching improved bluetooth technology offers near lossless audio quality audio quality at up to 328ft (100m)
